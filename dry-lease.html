‚úàDry Lease Fundamentals

Aircraft Only

In a dry lease arrangement, you receive the aircraft without crew, maintenance, or insurance. This provides maximum operational control and cost efficiency for established operators.

Financial Advantages

  • Lower monthly costs compared to wet lease
  • Off-balance sheet financing options
  • Tax advantages through operational leasing
  • Preserved capital for other investments
  • Flexible end-of-lease options

Operational Control

  • Your crew, your standards
  • Direct maintenance management
  • Custom aircraft configuration
  • Full scheduling flexibility
  • Brand and livery control

Market Dynamics

Fastest-Growing Segment

Dry lease market projected to reach $240.1B by 2034, driven by airline fleet expansion and capital efficiency needs.

Startup Success Model

Low-capex entry model enabling startups like Alhind Air (2025 launch) to enter markets quickly with reduced financial risk.
